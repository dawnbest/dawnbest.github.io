This landing page was an experiment in Sass (SCSS) following a tutorial that detailed how to create webpages that were responsive to multiple displays and devices.

Objectives

Overview: This project was created to provide a digital "business card" of sorts for people who are looking for small-scale 3D printing services in our area. This was a small experiment with streamlining the responsive site development process in Sass. The website layout changes when its window is either manually resized or shown on a smaller screen, such as a phone or tablet.
